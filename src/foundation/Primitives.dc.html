Primitives
The raw values Noor owns — the literal palette, spacing ladder, radii, type sizes and gradient recipes. These have no meaning on their own (they don't know what a "button" or "surface" is); they are the --noor-* and scale variables that Tokens give roles to. They are theme-independent: --noor-jade-green is always #00C950.
